The
Dotted
Ruler
Your all-in-one measurement tool.
Every ruler you ever needed - all in one tool.
The Dotted Ruler is an all-in-one measurement tool that combines the functions of a protractor, a ruler, a margin guide, and is designed with small circular holes with a .5 inch gap in-between.

Dotted Holes and Margin Guide

The ruler is laser-cut with small, tiny holes with half an inch gap in between. This makes more precise perspective drawing from different points with varying distances, all while being accurate in measurement.

Built-in Compass
and Protractor

Easily draw circles and angles with the built-in compass and protractor. Simply use two pens - one for the center and one for the radius - to draw circles, or use the protractor to measure angles accurately.

Portability and Convenience

The dotted ruler is an all-in-one ruler that encompasses the function of a compass, protractor, and many more functions into an A4-sized measurement tool that can be carried anywhere, anytime.
